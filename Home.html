DELHI to LEH perm_contact_calendar 11 March

We, discover Ladakh Adventure Tours & Travel based in Leh Ladakh is a certified Travel Company, recognized by Jammu & Kashmir Tourism department. welcome you to visit this beautiful part of India. Geographically surrounded by Tibet Border in North to South east and Pakistan border in North west and Kashmir valley in the west section of Leh. Down south section of Ladakh called Zanskar range which is the North section of Himachal Pradesh. Connected by air from Delhi to Leh regular flights and national highway from Manali to Leh and Srinagar to Leh. Though both highway may be close in winter season due to heavy snow fall at high passes.
